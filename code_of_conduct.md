# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
• Being respectful and inclusive
• Showing empathy towards other members
• Accepting constructive criticism gracefully

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the community leaders.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Leaders will follow guidelines to determine consequences for any action they deem in violation of this Code of Conduct.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant.
